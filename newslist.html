Fashion News: Dragon Ball x UNIQLO UT New Collaboration

The new collaboration between Dragon Ball and UNIQLO UT is about to be released, awakening memories of classic chapters.

Although the universe of the plot is still evolving, a significant part of the precious memories that the Japanese national manga Dragon Ball has left us comes from the classic chapters under the direction of Akira Toriyama.

The collaboration between UNIQLO UT and Dragon Ball has always been one of its ace series. This time, it brings together many elements from the "Earth Chapter" to the "Majin Buu Chapter" to create the most "authentic" Dragon Ball x UNIQLO UT collaboration series. Whether it's the arrangement of patterns or the emotional value, this collaboration series is full of desire to purchase.

Moreover, UNIQLO has also customized a 7-piece set for loyal fans, with a box that features the "summoning of the Shenron" through AR technology and comes with 7 stickers, which is quite sincere. The new collaboration between Dragon Ball and UNIQLO UT will be officially released on May 1st, so Dragon Ball fans should get ready.
